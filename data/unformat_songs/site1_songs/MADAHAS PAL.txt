MADAHAS PALAMA LYRICS

Madahas palama nagala ennata nokiya mata keewa
hithuwakkareta sitha nam athata kohedo piyamanna
denuwan chamara salena thaaleta rahasak ehi randa
methuwak athata ma gena awata danune na seema
sandakath rayakadi tharuka
saradam iwasaa
hirikada seethala gawila
adare danuna … danuna….

nawathinna no asa lamade ida dunne kawurundo
hitha dunne na innath pitupaa….
mata me tharam bara susumak boruwak kiyannata hakido
niskarane …. seema bandeewi aluthen

sansara dutu kale danunama huru purudu
ape as katha keruwa nawathee….
adahanna hakido kiyala mama maagenma ahapu
bawa danne na…. oba dannawada kiyala
