ME LOKE SIYALLO LYRICS

meloke siyallo sina wenne eido
beela yei kiya man hitha inna honda nea..
bonna yei kiyala mata bala karala
mea usas samaaje suraa paane pewwa..

ape me samaaje muthu menik bandeevee,
sudu endum adeevee, kalui sith oungea..
ayek lea pipaasen, suraawen neteemen,
suruupee landun den, labai sepa vinoden..

mage jeevithe mea gevee hondama kaalea,
renguu puhu vinode, denui mata denenne..
mulin gevapu kaale, aluth jeevithe mea,
venasa hondata there, palak nea ein nam

neteemen geyeemen, surawen vinoden,
hodak nam nowe in, lowe heti denea den..
meruna daata loke, nea netum vinode,
pem keru siyallan, damaa thaniwa yanne..
